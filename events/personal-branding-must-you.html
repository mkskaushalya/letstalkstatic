Spark Your Inner Self | Episode 05

Personal Branding! Must You? | 07-Sep-2022

Speakers

Mr. Kumar de Silva

Personality Development and
Corporate Etiquette Trainer

An outstanding personality in the country Mr. Kumar De Silva (Personality Development and Corporate Etiquette Trainer) conducted the session with the greatest perceptions on how to present yourself with the best personality traits. This session marked the closure of “Spark your Innerself”, inspiring our young industrialists to be more charismatic and outstanding on their own terms. A major impact of this session was that developing a strong personal brand can have several positive impacts on an individual`s career and personal life. Some benefits of mentioning personal branding include career advancement, better networking, and greater control over your reputation.
