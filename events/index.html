LETs talk 2022 Events

The IEEE Young Professionals Sri Lanka launched the LETs talk project in 2017 with the goal of enhancing the knowledge and development of professional skills of young professionals to become competent professionals in their future careers. This program is open to interested individuals with engineering backgrounds in addition to IEEE Young Professionals. The desired goals are realized by delivering sessions and workshops in collaboration with well-known individuals in the community on timely, important topics and focusing on the development of skills that are necessary for the growth of their careers. In addition, LETs talk unveiled most of their perceptive sessions collaborating with some prominent entities. Namely IEEE Sri Lanka Section Sight, IAS Sri Lanka Chapter and Luminary Learning Solutions Pvt Ltd.

The participants get the chance to hear from distinguished individuals who share their insider experiences in a wide range of fields. The presenters discuss opportunities, hurdles, mistakes, and success stories while offering advice on the common errors a novice professional could make during their first starting out. The flow is set up such that participants can consult the resource person after the event to seek clarifications and advice for their questions. For the sixth year running, LETs talk conducted two major programs in the year 2022 namely, a talk series “Spark Your Inner self” and “UpSkill 2022”. The IEEE LETs talk Organizing Committee for 2022, which was composed of 24 people, organized these sessions under the supervision of IEEE Young Professionals Sri Lanka. The IEEE Young Professionals Sri Lanka social media accounts, Facebook Page, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn accounts were used to promote these LETs talk sessions. Additionally, videos of the events were published on the IEEE Young Professionals Sri Lanka YouTube channel.

Spark Your Inner Self

Through the “Spark Your Inner self” talk series the LETs talk team brought out six presentations in 2022. This series was primarily focused on developing personal traits and crafting young industrialists into their best versions. This was a vital inspiration as well as a great motivation for the passionate young professionals who seek their future pathways to be more fruitful.

Due to the aftermath and present health guidelines due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the majority of the sessions were concluded as online sessions including the panel discussion.

Each session spanned for 1 and ½ hours. The following content presents the summary of the sessions we conducted throughout 2022 with a total participation of 850+, including 323 IEEE members and 532 Non-IEEE members.

UpSkill Sri Lanka 2022 focuses on overcoming a challenging period due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the “UpSkill 2022” was conducted as a hybrid event having two virtual sessions followed by a physical session. The event drew approximately 460 people, most attending both virtual and physical events with tremendous excitement.

The signature event was a blend of an event trio, a talk session, a panel discussion, and a workshop. Adapting to dynamic leadership concepts, developing management skills, and maintaining a positive image could be highlighted as the major outputs of the talk sessions. In contrast, the panel discussion delivered comprehensive insight to the potential growth of sustainable development and its vitality.

The last segment of the UpSkill 2022 was conducted as a physical session on the 6th of November 2022 at Trace Expert City with a participation of 77 delegates. The delegates physically engaged in varying activities to enhance the qualities and the proper behavior of a young professional in the workplace. The UpSkill remarked on the closure with a great representation of interpersonal skills.

SkillUp and Standout

The LETs Talk Organizing Committee conducted a talk series titled "Skill Up and Standout" that comprised four main presentations on different categories. The series was presented to an enthusiastic audience and covered topics such as "True Blue Strategy," "Logic Setup Creativity," "Pitch the Game," and "Growth Mindset: Idea behind continuous learning."

The talk series was held between the months of January and March in the year 2023, with each session spanning one and a half hours. It was a highly successful event series, which was a significant milestone in the journey of LETs Talk.
Overall, the LETs Talk Organizing Committee's Skill Up and Standout talk series provided valuable insights and knowledge to the attendees, helping them to enhance their skills and stand out in their respective fields.
