IEEE YP LETs talk

LETs Talk is a project started by IEEE Young Professionals Sri Lanka in 2017 with the goal of enhancing the knowledge of young professionals by giving talks in collaboration with prominent members of the society on timely key subjects and other skills that are required in shaping up their careers.

Get started

LETs Talk project mainly emphasizes the evolving soft skills of tech enthusiasts and educates students, professionals, and anybody with a desire to achieve greater heights, as denoted. Participants will have the opportunity to hear from eminent professionals who will offer their expert expertise in a variety of sectors.

The presenters highlight opportunities, hurdles, disappointments, and success stories, as well as illustrate words and jargon and offer advice on common rookie mistakes made by young professionals. The flow is set up in such a way that participants can have a post-speech conversation with the speaker to get their questions answered. Considering IEEE is the world‚Äôs largest technical professional organization, it clearly has the potential to provide opportunities for a significant number of young people. This program is open not only to IEEE Young Professionals, It will be a great opportunity for anybody who is enthusiastic and excited about engineering.

SkillUp and Standout

The LETs Talk Organizing Committee conducted a talk series titled "Skill Up and Standout" that comprised four main presentations on different categories. The series was presented to an enthusiastic audience and covered topics such as "True Blue Strategy," "Logic Setup Creativity," "Pitch the Game," and "Growth Mindset: Idea behind continuous learning."

The talk series was held between the months of January and March in the year 2023, with each session spanning one and a half hours. It was a highly successful event series, which was a significant milestone in the journey of LETs Talk.
Overall, the LETs Talk Organizing Committee's Skill Up and Standout talk series provided valuable insights and knowledge to the attendees, helping them to enhance their skills and stand out in their respective fields.
